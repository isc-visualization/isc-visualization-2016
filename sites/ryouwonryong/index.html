"U.S. Supreme Court Justices Being Liberal?"

[◀] [▶]

About:
Each rectangle represents a justice, sorted from left to right with the 'liberal' for an year. Hover your mouse over those, and you can see additional information. Check left and right side for what year you are watching and who the highlighted justice is. Shattered block means start/end year of an justice. If you still don't get there is not only one chart and can't change it, I'm pleased to inform you that clicking [◀] or [▶] makes desired change.

All the charts are showing that U.S. supreme court has certain trend to get liberal, rather than conservative. Not only for nowadays, it's quite a meta-trend. Take a look for those charts, and see how those charts persuade you.
